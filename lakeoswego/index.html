Conservation Strategies

It is important for residents in this area to not only understand the vital role that Oregon white oaks play, but also how to keep them healthy. Mature oaks provide many valuable ecosystem services, but most oak trees do not reach maturity in the wild and the survival rate is even lower in urban environments. One major step conservationists can take is monitoring for and removal of invasive species, which drive out native plants and can overcrowd growing oak saplings.
